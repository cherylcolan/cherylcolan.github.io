Quality ingredients, radiant results

Le Collagene is exclusively formulated to provide your skin with the ultimate in moisture rejuvenation, giving your skin a silky feel. From the moment you apply Le Collagene to your skin, you will notice its lightweight texture, quick absorption and smooth feel.

In today’s often hectic and fast-paced world, your complexion can be exposed to many stressors that affect its resilience and health. Pollution, chemicals and overexposure to the elements can all leave your skin dry, flaky and dull looking.

Le Collagene’s exclusive blend of ingredients contain HYDROLYZED COLLAGEN, which increases the skin’s hydration levels, and HYROLYZED ELASTIN, which enables the skin to retain moisture and prevent dryness. SALICYLIC ACID acts as an exfoliant to gentl y remove dead skin cells and make room for new cell growth, resulting in a refreshed and revitalized complexion. VITAMIN A, a powerful antioxidant, works to improve the texture, smoothness and firmness of your skin. VITAMIN C is responsible for the production of collagen in your skin and acts as a ca talyst for the neutralization of free radicals that can damage and age your skin prematurely.
