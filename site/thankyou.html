5 simple ways to live a better life

1. Stay hydrated

Drink 8 glasses of purified water every day to keep your complexion clear and your body feeling great.

2. Get moving

Take a 30 minute walk three times a week to brighten your skin and work your muscles.

3. Give thanks

Taking time to count your blessings and practice gratitude will put a smile on your face and a spring in your step.

4. Be generous

Open a door, give a smile or stop and listen to a friend. As we give to others we lower our stress and increase our enjoyment of life.

5. Chill out

Set aside a small portion of your day to breathe deeply, clear your thoughts and simply relax.
