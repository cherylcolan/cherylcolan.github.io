Top 3 Ways to Get Your Fruit On!

Malabar Tamarind

You may not have heard of the tamarind fruit, but you may have noticed the media buzz about the benefits of garcinia cambogia, the fruit of the tamarind. Recent studies have shown there may be a link between a key ingredient of garcinia cambogia; hydroxycitric acid and losing weight fast. Some researchers have suggested that hydroxycitric acid (hca) when taken in concentrations of 50% or higher can be a powerful fat burner, an energy booster and a key to quick weight loss!     Learn More »

Grapefruit

Pucker up! Packed with a powerful dose of Vitamin C, these tart fruits provide excellent support to your immune system. Vitamin C helps to fight free radical damage that can lead to dangerous inflammation, high cholesterol and heart disease. Grapefruits also contain an important phytonutrient called lycopene, which has been shown to be beneficial in reducing a man’s risk for prostate cancer. In addition, the pectin contained in grapefruits is a form of soluble fiber that helps to lower your cholesterol.

Kiwifruit

Small but mighty, kiwifruits are loaded with phytonutrients, vitamins and minerals all designed to fight disease, improve cardiovascular function and strengthen the immune system. Kiwifruits are an excellent source of the powerful antioxidant Vitamin C, which neutralizes dangerous free radicals that destroy cells and wreak havoc on the body. A good source of dietary fiber, kiwifruits can play a significant role in regulating blood sugar, removing bad cholesterol and improving heart health.
